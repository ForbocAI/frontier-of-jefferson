# Frontier of Jefferson

This packet is a county road book for a place that never finished being settled. The pages that follow hold the people, the land, the pressure, and the few rules needed to ride into 1899 Jefferson State with a posse of your own. Every section is a road, a job, or a debt waiting to be ridden. Open where the trouble calls you.

## Header

The site header carries the packet title — **Frontier of Jefferson** — and a single return link to this index. Keep it narrow as a saddle cinch: the title on the left, the home trail on the right, nothing else but the thin rule below.

## Footer

The site footer holds the project name, the year of record (1899), and a short attribution line. No decoration beyond a single rule above. It should close each page the way a county clerk closes a ledger: quietly, with the date.
